ACC-BD-NSU AI-SAT CubeSat Conceptual and Preliminary Design Report

Program: APSCO Student Small Satellite Project · Year: 2024 · Team: NSU Spacecraft Lab

This preliminary design report was submitted as part of the Asia-Pacific Space Cooperation Organization (APSCO) Student Small Satellite (SSS) Project. The report covers the conceptual and preliminary design of a 3U CubeSat mission for Earth observation and technology demonstration.

Key Topics: CubeSat Design, Mission Analysis, ADCS Subsystem, Payload Design, Communication Systems
