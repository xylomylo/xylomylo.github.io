About

I am an MSc Computer Science (AI) student at the University of Nottingham, UK, with research interests spanning Robotics, Dynamical Control Systems, Computer Vision, Machine Learning, and Biomedical Engineering.

Previously, I completed my BSc in Electrical & Electronic Engineering at North South University, Bangladesh (CGPA: 3.53/4.0, Cum Laude), with focus areas in Robotics and VLSI Design. I have hands-on experience in attitude control systems for spacecraft, quantum computing simulations, and embedded systems design.

Beyond research, I bring leadership experience as the founding president of Bangladesh's first school robotics club and as the 27th President of NSU Young Entrepreneurs Society.
